about us

Axis Architecture is an Austin-based design firm with a focus in both architecture and interior design. We are a internationally award-winning practice that works closely with our clients to create timeless designs. Our scope of work includes but is not limited to adaptive reuse projects, commercial, hospitality, mixed-use developments, multifamily housing, and retail design. Our work has been recognized across the United States and internationally for our innovative designs.
